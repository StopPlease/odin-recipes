Fruit Shots

It's Jello with a kick. Vodka is mixed with any fruit flavored gelatin and drunk in a shot glass.

Not for Kids.

Ingredients

  • 1 package fruit flavored Jell-O mix
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1.25 cups vodka, chilled
  • 3/4 cup cold water

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix boiling water with gelatin. Stir until gelatin dissolves. Stir in vodka and cold water. Pour mixture into plastic shot glasses and chill in refrigerator for 1 hour, or until set.
  2. Once set, remove and enjoy.
